About Xfoot

Our mission to revolutionize amateur football

Our Story

Xfoot was founded with a simple vision: to make amateur football more accessible, organized, and enjoyable for everyone. We recognized that connecting players, organizing matches, and managing leagues was unnecessarily complicated.

Today, Xfoot is the leading platform for amateur football enthusiasts, helping thousands of players and organizers connect, compete, and build communities around the beautiful game.

Our Mission

To empower amateur football players and organizers by providing a seamless, intuitive platform that connects communities, simplifies match organization, and enables league management at scale.

Our Values

Community First

We believe in the power of community and building connections through football.

Simplicity

We make complex processes simple and intuitive for everyone.

Inclusivity

Football is for everyone, and our platform reflects that commitment.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from product to service.

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website or use our application. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you use Xfoot to improve your experience.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Performance Cookies

Functional Cookies
